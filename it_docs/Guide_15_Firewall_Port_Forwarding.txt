Category: Networking and Communications
Title: Setting Up Safe Inbound Firewall Port Forwarding Rules
ID: NET_05
Symptoms: Internal servers hosting services cannot receive connection requests originating from external networks.
Resolution:
1. Access the enterprise edge firewall management portal.
2. Assign a strict reservation or static IP address to the internal destination server.
3. Create an inbound rule defining the exact external port number and corresponding internal port number.
4. Choose the specific protocol type required by the application: TCP, UDP, or both.
5. Bind the forwarding rule explicitly to the incoming WAN interface.
6. Use an external port scanning service to confirm the port status shows as open and responsive.
