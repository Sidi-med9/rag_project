Category: Operating Systems
Title: Resetting SMC and NVRAM on Intel-based Mac Computers
ID: OS_09
Symptoms: Abnormal cooling fan speeds, power management issues, battery charging failures, or display resolution resets.
Resolution:
1. Shut down the Mac completely.
2. To reset NVRAM: Press the power button, then hold Option + Command + P + R keys immediately for 20 seconds.
3. Release the keys after hearing the second startup chime or seeing the Apple logo twice.
4. To reset SMC on laptops with non-removable batteries: Shut down, hold Shift + Control + Option on the left side, then press the power button.
5. Hold all keys down for 10 seconds, release them, and turn on the Mac normally.
