Category: Security and Accounts
Title: Investigating and Handling Suspected Phishing Emails
ID: SEC_05
Symptoms: Employees receive high-urgency emails mimicking executive staff or external banking platforms, demanding immediate credential confirmation.
Resolution:
1. Advise users never to open attached documents or click hyperlinks embedded inside suspicious messages.
2. Open the email security administration console and search for the specific email message ID.
3. Inspect email header configurations, validating Sender Policy Framework (SPF), DKIM, and DMARC alignments.
4. If spoofing signatures are confirmed, flag the sender's domain global block list.
5. Run a tenant-wide purge operation to remove matching messages out of all active employee mailboxes.
