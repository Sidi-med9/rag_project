Category: Networking and Communications
Title: Mitigating Wireless Signal Interference in Office Spaces
ID: NET_06
Symptoms: Wi-Fi networks suffer from high packet loss, slow data rates, and random drops despite close proximity to access points.
Resolution:
1. Run a Wi-Fi analyzer tool to audit channel occupancy across nearby networks.
2. For 2.4 GHz deployments, explicitly configure access points to operate on non-overlapping channels: 1, 6, or 11.
3. Move high-bandwidth devices over to the 5 GHz or 6 GHz bands to alleviate channel saturation.
4. Identify and isolate physical interference sources such as unshielded power lines, brick walls, or microwave machinery.
5. Adjust transmission power metrics on adjacent access points to minimize overlapping cell interference.
